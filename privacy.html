1. Who we are

Antri is a job-search workspace for tracking applications, follow-ups, and replies, available at antri.xyz. Antri is also open-source software you can self-host; this policy covers the hosted service we operate. If you self-host Antri, you are the data controller for your own instance.

2. What we collect

We collect only what's needed to run the service:

  • Account data — your email address, and (if you use Google sign-in) basic Google profile/authentication details. Authentication is handled by our provider, Supabase.
  • Application data — the job-tracking records you create: role, company, location, dates, status, priority, compensation, source, job URL, contacts, follow-up dates, and notes.
  • Job-posting content — when you use Smart Add or the browser extension, the page URL, title, and visible text of a posting you choose to save.
  • Payment data — if you subscribe to Antri Pro, your subscription status and billing identifiers. Card details are entered directly with Stripe; we never see or store your full card number.
  • Operational data — basic logs and error diagnostics needed to keep the service running and secure.

3. How we use it

We use the data above to authenticate your account, store and sync your application records, extract job-posting details when you ask us to, show your tracking views and insights, send service emails you've enabled (such as follow-up digests), process subscriptions, diagnose failures, and protect the service from abuse.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use your application data or extension page data for advertising.

4. The browser extension

The Antri Job Saver extension only reads the active web page after you click Save. For that page it may collect the URL, title, visible job text, and structured job-posting data, and send it over HTTPS to Antri to build a draft you review before saving.

To verify your subscription, the extension reads your own signed-in Antri session token from antri.xyz and sends it with the request. It does not run on, read, or transmit data from any other website, and it does not scan your browsing activity.

5. AI extraction

When AI extraction is enabled, the text of a job posting you choose to process may be sent to our AI provider (OpenAI) to convert it into structured fields. This happens only for postings you actively submit. AI output can be wrong — you always review the draft before it is saved.

7. Payments

Antri Pro subscriptions are billed through Stripe. When you subscribe, you enter your payment details directly with Stripe and they are processed under Stripe's terms and privacy policy. Antri stores only your subscription status and the identifiers needed to manage your plan (for example, a Stripe customer ID). See our Terms for billing, trial, and cancellation details.

8. Google user data

If you use Google sign-in, Antri uses the Google authentication information needed to create and access your account. Antri's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements. We do not use Google user data for advertising and do not sell it.

9. Cookies & local storage

Antri uses your browser's local storage to keep you signed in (your session token) and to remember some preferences. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies. The browser extension stores your session token locally so it can authenticate your saves.

10. Retention

We keep your account and application data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete your application records and account, and cancel any active subscription. Some records may persist briefly in encrypted backups or where retention is required for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention reasons (for example, payment records held by Stripe).

11. Your rights & choices

  • Access & export — export all of your application data at any time as JSON or CSV from inside Antri.
  • Correct — edit any application record directly.
  • Delete — delete individual records, or delete your entire account and data from your account menu (Account → Delete account).
  • Unsubscribe — turn off or unsubscribe from service emails such as the follow-up digest.
  • Remove the extension — uninstall the Chrome extension at any time.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (such as under GDPR or CCPA) to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. Contact us to exercise them.

12. Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored with per-user access controls so you can only read and write your own records. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data and limit who and what can access it.

13. Children

Antri is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

14. Changes

We may update this policy as Antri evolves. We'll change the "last updated" date above, and for material changes we'll provide a more prominent notice. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.
