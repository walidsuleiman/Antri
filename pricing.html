FAQ

Questions, answered

Is Antri really free?

Yes. The full tracker is open-source and free to use forever — on the cloud at antri.xyz or self-hosted from the GitHub repo. You only pay if you want Antri Pro's AI automation, cloud extension, and cross-device sync.

What exactly does the $9.99/mo Pro plan add?

Pro unlocks AI automation, the cloud-synced browser extension, and cloud storage tied to your account — so your pipeline syncs automatically across every device you sign in on, with nothing for you to host or maintain.

Can I self-host instead of paying?

Absolutely. Antri is dependency-free HTML/CSS/JS with a small Python backend. Clone the repo, run it locally or on your own cloud, and you keep the full tracker at no cost. Your data stays in your own storage.

Where is my data stored?

On the free self-hosted setup, your data lives wherever you host it. On Antri Pro, it's saved to your account in managed cloud storage so it syncs across devices. Either way, you can export everything to JSON or CSV anytime.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel whenever you like — and because your applications are always exportable, you can move back to the free self-hosted version without losing anything.
