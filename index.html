Why it's called Antri

Ants don't lose track. Neither should you.

The name Antri comes from ants — nature's most organized, persistent, and systematic foragers. A single ant leaves a trail; the colony turns thousands of tiny, scattered actions into one coordinated system that always finds its way back.

A job search works exactly the same way. Small actions — apply, follow up, log a reply — add up. Antri is the trail that keeps them organized so nothing gets lost. Follow it down this page.

Everything in the box

Built to do the boring tracking for you.

From capturing a posting to knowing what to do next — Antri covers the whole loop.

Smart Add

Paste a job link and Antri prefills the role, company, location, compensation, source, and notes into a ready-to-review draft.

Browser saver extension

One click on LinkedIn, Indeed, or Workday captures the rendered posting and opens a prefilled draft — even on sites that block fetching.

Pipeline insights

See total applications, active opportunities, replies, and due follow-ups — plus status distribution and which sources respond.

Follow-up tracking

Upcoming and overdue actions stay front and center, so the right nudge happens at the right moment.

Search, filter & sort

Find any role instantly by company, status, source, location, or notes. Sort by date, priority, or follow-up.

Import & export

Your data is portable. Export to JSON or CSV anytime, and import Antri JSON to pick up where you left off.

How it works

Three steps from chaos to pipeline.

STEP 01

Capture

Paste a job link, use the browser extension, or add a role by hand. Antri builds a structured draft in seconds.

STEP 02

Organize

Set status, priority, source, and a follow-up date. Every application lands in one clean, searchable pipeline.

STEP 03

Act

Antri surfaces what's due and what's working, so you follow up on time and lean into the sources that reply.
